About Me

Hi! Hi! Call me Sharkey! I'm an 18 y/o artist attending college, hoping to get commissions to pay for said college! I've been drawing for awhile; I also figured commissions could keep me motivated to draw, as it's something I love to do and I find it incredibly calming

About Commissions

When asking about commissions, please have an idea of what you want, whether it's an already made character or a Pinterest board as inspiration, as well as what the commission will be, i.e., a headshot, bust, full body pose (preferably a pose/pose reference accompanied), and if possible, a color sheet!

Rules/Guidelines

I will not do anything NSFW, as in, like porn or overtly sexual anything like that; nothing hateful/racist/bigoted/etc. Be nice and be patient. I am a full-time college student, but I'm trying my best! I will send a sketch once a sketch is finished and ask what you think, unless you'd rather keep it a surprise. At that point, adjustments are allowed, but nothing incredibly major! But after that point, little to no adjustments can be made!

Pricing

I'm flexible, and new to this so I was thinking around $17 CAD per hour! I only accept paypal and it will be given once commissioned.
